# Site photography

The site uses three placeholder photos from Unsplash. All are free to use under the Unsplash license — no attribution required, but credit is welcome.

## Current files

| File | Used on | Source | Suggested replacement |
|---|---|---|---|
| `hero-desk.jpg` | Homepage hero (right column) | Unsplash | Your own workspace, hands-at-keyboard, or a quiet object photo |
| `notebook.jpg` | About page side panel | Unsplash | A photo of your handwriting, notebook, or something personal |
| `services-texture.jpg` | Services page accent band | Unsplash | A quiet architectural or environmental photo from Jubail / KSA |

## Swapping in your own photos

1. Save your photo as a JPG with the **same filename** as the placeholder you want to replace.
2. Aspect ratios to roughly preserve:
   - `hero-desk.jpg` — landscape, ~3:2 (1500×1000 or larger)
   - `notebook.jpg` — landscape, ~4:3 (1600×1200 or larger)
   - `services-texture.jpg` — wide landscape, ~16:9 (2000×1125 or larger)
3. Keep files under ~500 KB. Use https://squoosh.app if needed to compress.
4. `git add` the file, commit, and push. Vercel auto-deploys.

A portrait of yourself on `/about` is intentionally not used yet — send one when you're ready and I'll add it.
