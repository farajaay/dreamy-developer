# Maintenance & reliability maturity assessment

**Use:** to assess where a site's maintenance and reliability practices stand
against good practice, and to lay out what to fix first. Combine document
review, CMMS data analysis, walkdowns, and interviews across maintenance,
operations, and planning.

**Maturity scale (1–5):**
1. Reactive · 2. Planned · 3. Proactive · 4. Reliability-centred · 5. Optimised.
Score each dimension, evidence the score, and note the realistic next level.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is criticality assessed, and does it actually change how you maintain each asset?
- Where does most maintenance effort go — planned work, or chasing failures?
- Can you name your top 5 bad actors and what's being done about each?
- Is CMMS data trusted enough to make decisions, or worked around?
- What stops a good PM from being done on time — labour, spares, access, or production?
- When something fails twice, does anyone formally ask why?
