# Client intake & discovery questionnaire

**Use:** the first structured conversation with a prospective client, before any
scope or price is committed. Fill it in live on the call where you can; send it
ahead for the factual sections. Goal: understand the problem the way an operator
does, and leave the call able to write a clear scope.
