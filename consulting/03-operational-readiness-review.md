# Operational readiness review (ORR)

**Use:** before startup / handover from a project (or after a major change) to
test whether the people, procedures, systems, spares, and documentation are
genuinely ready to operate and maintain the asset safely from day one. Run it
early enough that gaps can still be closed.

**Status per item:** `Ready` · `In progress` · `Gap` · `N/A`. Note owner and
target date for every non-`Ready` item.

## 2. Training & competence
- [ ] Operators trained on the actual system (not just the manual) — `[ ]`
- [ ] Maintenance trained on the installed equipment — `[ ]`
- [ ] Emergency, isolation, and abnormal-situation training done — `[ ]`
- [ ] Training records complete and auditable — `[ ]`

## 3. Procedures & documentation
- [ ] Standard operating procedures written and validated — `[ ]`
- [ ] Start-up, shutdown, and emergency procedures in place — `[ ]`
- [ ] Maintenance procedures and OEM manuals available on-site/in CMMS — `[ ]`
- [ ] As-built drawings, P&IDs, and control narratives handed over — `[ ]`
- [ ] Permit-to-work and isolation (LOTO) procedures live — `[ ]`

## 4. Systems & tools
- [ ] Control & safety systems commissioned, tested, and signed off — `[ ]`
- [ ] CMMS loaded with asset register, PMs, and BOMs — `[ ]`
- [ ] Historian / reporting configured and verified — `[ ]`
- [ ] Alarms rationalised and not in flood at startup — `[ ]`

## 5. Spares & materials
- [ ] Critical spares identified, procured, and stored — `[ ]`
- [ ] Min/max levels set in inventory system — `[ ]`
- [ ] Long-lead and OEM-only items secured — `[ ]`
- [ ] Consumables and first-fill chemicals/lubricants on-site — `[ ]`

## 6. Safety, environment & compliance
- [ ] HAZOP/PHA actions closed or risk-accepted with sign-off — `[ ]`
- [ ] Safety systems (F&G, ESD, relief) tested and proven — `[ ]`
- [ ] Statutory inspections / certifications obtained — `[ ]`
- [ ] Environmental permits and monitoring in place — `[ ]`
- [ ] Emergency response plan written, resourced, and drilled — `[ ]`

## 7. Maintenance readiness
- [ ] Preventive-maintenance plans built and scheduled — `[ ]`
- [ ] Equipment criticality assessed; strategy matched to it — `[ ]`
- [ ] Special tools and test equipment available — `[ ]`
- [ ] Maintenance contracts / OEM support agreements in place — `[ ]`

## 8. Interfaces & support
- [ ] Vendor support and warranty terms documented and accessible — `[ ]`
- [ ] IT/OT support arrangements defined — `[ ]`
- [ ] Utilities and feedstock supply confirmed and contracted — `[ ]`
- [ ] Defined punch-list and process to clear residual project items — `[ ]`

## 9. Handover & governance
- [ ] Clear, dated handover criteria agreed between project and operations — `[ ]`
- [ ] Operations has formally accepted (or conditionally accepted) the asset — `[ ]`
- [ ] Open actions tracked with owners; go/no-go decision basis defined — `[ ]`
