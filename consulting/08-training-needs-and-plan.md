# Training needs analysis & session plan

**Use:** to scope and design training that changes behaviour on the plant, not
training that fills a day. Two parts: a needs analysis (what gap are we closing,
and how will we know it closed) and a session/programme plan.

## Common workshop topics (off-the-shelf starting points)
- OT / ICS fundamentals and cybersecurity awareness for plant teams
- Reading and using control narratives, P&IDs, and loop diagrams
- Maintenance strategy: PM vs PdM vs CBM, and matching to criticality
- Defect elimination and structured root-cause analysis
- Turnaround planning discipline and scope control
- Operational readiness and safe handover practice
