# Retainer Agreement — Studio Engagement

> **[REVIEW REQUIRED]** Working draft. Have a Saudi-qualified lawyer review before signing.

This Retainer Agreement ("**Agreement**") is entered into as of **{{effective_date}}** under the Master Services Agreement between **Ahmad Yousif AlFaraj** ("**Consultant**") and **{{client_legal_name}}** ("**Client**") dated **{{msa_date}}** (the "**MSA**"). Capitalised terms used and not defined here have the meaning given in the MSA.

## 1. Engagement

The Client engages the Consultant on a continuing retainer basis to provide development, advisory, and related professional services (the "**Retainer Services**"). The Retainer Services are not limited to a fixed scope; they are directed by the Client's evolving priorities, agreed jointly each month.

## 2. Term

2.1 This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and runs for an initial term of **three (3) months** (the "**Initial Term**").

2.2 After the Initial Term, this Agreement automatically renews on a **month-to-month** basis until terminated under Section 8.

## 3. Retainer Hours

3.1 The Consultant will commit up to **{{e.g., eighty (80)}} working hours per calendar month** to the Retainer Services (the "**Retainer Hours**"). For reference, this corresponds to approximately ten (10) working days per month.

3.2 The Consultant will provide a written log of hours worked, by category, with each monthly invoice.

3.3 **Carryover.** Unused Retainer Hours do not carry over to the following month, except that up to **{{e.g., ten (10)}} hours** may be carried forward by mutual written agreement during a slower month.

3.4 **Overage.** If the Client requests work that would exceed the Retainer Hours in a given month, the Consultant will notify the Client in writing before exceeding the cap. Additional hours, if agreed, are billed at **SAR {{hourly_overage_rate}} per hour**, exclusive of VAT.

## 4. Fees

4.1 **Monthly retainer fee:** **SAR {{monthly_fee}}** per month, exclusive of VAT (15%).

4.2 **Invoicing.** The Consultant will issue an invoice on the first business day of each month for that month's fee. Invoices are payable within fourteen (14) calendar days of issue.

4.3 **Pro-ration.** The first and final months may be pro-rated if this Agreement begins or ends mid-month.

4.4 **Annual review.** Fees will be reviewed annually and may be adjusted by written agreement reflecting changes in scope, market rates, or KSA inflation. Either Party may propose a fee change at least sixty (60) days before the proposed effective date.

## 5. Scope and Priorities

5.1 At the start of each calendar month, the Parties will agree (by email is sufficient) the **monthly priorities** — a brief list of the work items the Consultant will focus on that month.

5.2 The Client may reprioritise during the month with reasonable notice. The Consultant will accommodate where practical, and will flag any impact on previously-agreed items.

5.3 Work outside the broad fields of software development, technical advisory, and process automation is outside the scope of this Agreement.

## 6. Reporting

The Consultant will provide a **weekly written progress report** of no more than one page covering: (a) what was done that week; (b) what is planned for the next week; (c) any blockers; and (d) hours used to date in the month.

## 7. Pause

7.1 Either Party may pause this Agreement for up to **one (1) month** in any rolling twelve-month period by giving fourteen (14) days' written notice.

7.2 During a pause, no monthly fee is invoiced and no Retainer Hours are provided. This Agreement otherwise continues, and Sections 8, 9, and 10 of the MSA (confidentiality, IP, data protection) remain in full force.

7.3 The Initial Term is extended by the duration of any pause.

## 8. Termination

8.1 After the Initial Term, either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on **thirty (30) days' written notice**.

8.2 During the Initial Term, this Agreement may be terminated only for material breach not cured within fifteen (15) days of written notice, or as otherwise permitted under Section 13 of the MSA.

8.3 **Effects of termination.** On termination: (a) the Client will pay any outstanding fees and any pre-approved expenses incurred up to the termination date; (b) the Consultant will deliver all completed Work Product for which fees have been paid, plus a written handover note covering the state of any in-progress work; and (c) Sections 6, 8, 9, 11, and 16 of the MSA survive.

## 9. Exclusivity and Conflicts

9.1 This Agreement is **non-exclusive**. The Consultant may take on other clients during the Term.

9.2 The Consultant will not knowingly take on work for a direct competitor of the Client within the Client's primary market segment without the Client's prior written consent. *(`[REVIEW REQUIRED]` — define "direct competitor" if the Client requires.)*

## 10. Relationship to MSA

This Agreement is governed by, and subject to, the terms of the MSA. In case of conflict, the MSA prevails unless this Agreement expressly overrides a specific MSA clause by reference.
